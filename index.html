Crystal Collector

Instructions

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • By clicking on a crystal you will add a speciif amount of points to your total score.
  • Figure out the value by clicking on a crystal. Each click/value will be added to total score.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden and will change with each round.
  • You must match your total score to the random number to win. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.
  • Good luck!
